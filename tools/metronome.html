How to use this metronome

Set your tempo with the slider or the +/minus buttons, choose a time signature, and hit Start (or press the spacebar). The first beat of each bar is accented so you can feel the pulse.

Practising with a metronome

  • Start slower than feels comfortable — accuracy first, speed later.
  • Raise the tempo in small steps (2 to 4 BPM) once a passage is clean.
  • Practise difficult bars in isolation before playing the whole piece.
