How the tuner works

Click Enable microphone and allow access. Play a single note clearly — the tuner shows the nearest note, the exact frequency, and how many cents you are sharp or flat. Aim for the centre line; within +/- 5 cents counts as in tune.

Standard tuning reference

  • Guitar: E2, A2, D3, G3, B3, E4
  • Bass: E1, A1, D2, G2
  • Ukulele: G4, C4, E4, A4

Detection runs entirely in your browser — your audio is never uploaded to any server.
